Customize how auth emails look from the plugin: **Settings > Branding** tab.

## Settings

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **App Name** | Shown in email headers |
| **Logo URL** | Your logo displayed in emails |
| **Primary Color** | Button and link color in emails |
| **Button Text Color** | Text color on buttons in emails |
| **Support Email** | Shown in email footers |

## Affected Emails

All transactional emails use your branding:

- **Welcome** — Sent after registration
- **Password Reset** — Sent from Forgot Password form
- **Magic Link** — Sent for passwordless sign-in
- **OTP Code** — Sent for code verification

## Preview

Switch to the **Email Preview** tab in Settings to see how your branding looks before publishing.

<Tip>
Use a square or horizontal logo (recommended: 200x50px). Host it on your domain or a CDN for reliable loading.
</Tip>
