---
title: "Installation"
description: "Install Auth Plugin from the Framer Marketplace."
icon: "download"
---

1. Open your Framer project
2. Go to **Plugins** (left sidebar or `Cmd/Ctrl + /`)
3. Search **"Auth Plugin"**
4. Click **Install**
5. Open the plugin from the Plugins panel

<Warning>
Your site must be published at least once before running setup. The plugin needs a published URL to configure properly.
</Warning>
